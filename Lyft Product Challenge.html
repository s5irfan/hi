Lyft Product Challenge

Problem Definition 



Commuting to work can be very time consuming and expensive to most passengers who ride a train or bus to get to work everyday. From my personal experience, this is the hardest part of the day, commuting for long hours and by the time you get home its almost time to sleep. The routine starts with you have to wake up early in the morning to catch the train and god forbid you miss your train there goes another 30 minutes waiting for the train and now you are most definitely late for work. On average, I pay about $555 per month to get to work. It takes $60/month to drive from my house to the train station and parking at the train station costs $180/month. It takes about $315/month to travel all the way to work. Finally, it takes about 5-7 minutes walking to my work place. 



American households spent an average of $9,503 on transit needs in 2015, the most recent year for which the Bureau of Labor Statistics published its comprehensive survey of consumer expenditures. This a ridiculous  amount of money spent on just transportations. How can we reduce the amount of money spent on transportation?

Solution - Lyft Reliable Drivers Program

The purpose of the Lyft Reliable Drivers Program is to bring cheap rides to people who commute to work every day. We need to have dedicated drivers that are willing to drive passengers to their work places regularly. This is where Lyft can be helpful and provide passengers with cheap rides and guaranteeing drivers a steady income. These passengers are looking for committed long term drivers who they can rely on for a ride. 


As a passenger of this program I will be able to save $415. As a driver I will be able to make $140 per month and will have reliable passengers without uncertainty of not having enough rides leading to a low income. Both the passenger and driver have great benefits with this program

Types of Users

There will be two main users of the app drivers and passengers. These users will be the frequent users of the application. The feature design will primarily solve needs of the users below: 

There will be 3 types of passengers:

Corporate Workers - These users have fixed timings to work and will be using this service more frequently than other users.

Students: These users will typically have fixed timings and can use a scheduled driver. These users can be divided into 5 categories:
  • Preschool 
  • Elementary School
  • Middle School 
  • High School
  • University
Part Time Workers - These users will have variation in their timings. Having a weekly scheduled driver would be beneficial to them, as their scheduled cannot be predicted easily.

Drivers - These users will be committed drivers who are willing to take passengers on scheduled rides.

Metrics

Metrics of the feature will be shown through google analytics. This will help us understand how effective this feature is and are users able to understand the process. We can determine areas of improvement and how to better serve our users.

Lyft can track the following KPI’s
  • How many users are actively using this feature. This will help understand if this feature is improving Lyft's  overall user growth.
  • How much time the user has spent per session on the specific feature?
  • Did Drivers income increased significantly after the use of the feature?
  • How much % of driver income is coming from this feature?
